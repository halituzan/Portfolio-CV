About

I was only 10 - 11 years old when I first met the computer. My uncle bought me a computer and all I could do on that computer with Windows 98 was to draw something in paint. There was no mouse on the computer, and I memorized almost all the shortcuts that could be used.

After that, when I was 15-16 years old, I met the internet. Internet cafes had just become popular where I lived, and I was going to play games at that time, I got caught up in MMORPG games, which I can call the plague of that era. It took me a while to recover :)

Then I met a site that provides free hosting in Turkey. Mynet had a free platform called Sitemynet. I created a domain name on this site, believe me, I did not know what to do, but I created it. Then I started adding pictures and texts to this site, I liked it very much. After dealing with this for a long time, I started to wonder about the background of websites. Flash websites, which were the trending designs of that period, caught my attention and started to make changes on them by finding flash site themes, but although flash sites can be very stylish, they limit me.

While researching on the internet, I learned that there is an infrastructure called wordpress and that it is an open source structure. I wanted to swim in an unfamiliar sea and put the flash aside and entered the PHP-based Wordpress world. After struggling with Wordpress for a while, while preparing for the university exams (I know it's a big mistake), unfortunately, while preparing for the university exams, I prepared by thinking of getting away from this world and guaranteeing my future.

During my university life, when I said that this course is yours and this course is mine, I couldn't go a long way in software. After my university life was over, I couldn't deal with software again due to work problems.

Finally, after 2016, I try to make new discoveries by focusing on fully oriented software languages.
